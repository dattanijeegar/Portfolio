About

Jeegar Dattani is a professional cricket presenter, commentator, formal events emcee, national and international speaker at various professional fora.

Cricket Presenter & Commentator.

  • He has been a cricket commentator for several leagues in Maharashtra, Gujarat and Bangkok.
  • He has been a speaker on large professional platforms across India and 9 countries around the world.
  • With a medical and management background, wide healthcare industry experience across different cultures and countries including South East Asia, Australia, Africa, he brings a unique professional style in his speaking roles as a presenter, commentator and speaker.
  • Jeegar is a trained voiceover artist from Mumbai, India and holds a professional diploma in Medical Journalism. With professional engagements spanning from medical media house to serving as faculty for medical communications’ courses, he is well-placed to be able to add flavors of media and journalism in his speaking assignments.
